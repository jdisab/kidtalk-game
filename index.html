Therapist-friendly reminders

Low-demand: Let the child pass, point, draw, choose from examples, or answer with a metaphor.

Autistic-affirming: Do not force eye contact, emotional labeling, or “why” answers.

Safety: If a card raises risk, pause the game and follow your clinical/safety protocol.
