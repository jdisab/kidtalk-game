Therapist Card Game

KidTalk Cards

A colorful flashcard-style question game to help kids share thoughts, feelings, and stories.

For therapists, counselors, and helpers. Not a diagnostic tool.

Pick your card pile

Ready
Choose one or more card piles, then draw a card.
Tip: Kids can answer with words, pointing, drawing, animals, colors, movement, or “pass.”

Therapist notes

Let the child skip any card. The goal is expression, not perfect answers.

For autistic kids, concrete choices can help: animal, color, weather, body feeling, drawing, or choosing from examples.
